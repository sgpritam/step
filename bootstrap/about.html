Introduction

Today, the field of forensic science is becoming a great career option for the students who wants to contribute in developing a crime free nation and a safer place to live in. Not only scholars but law professionals like paralegals, attorneys, corporate employees and investigators are also showing interest in the trainings so to excel in their respective fields.

Forensic system is considered as the backbone of the justice system and plays a crucial role in collecting scientific information which further helps in the ongoing investigations. Forensic experts have the ability to maximize the value of the evidence presented in the court. Forensic science is a vital field which find its applications in security agencies, IT companies, banks, investigation bureaus, CBI, hospitals, multinational companies, etc. With the evolution of modern technology, the methods of committing crime is also changed drastically thus, it is important for the investigators to keep their knowledge up-to-date. Further, forensic science is applicable in the examination of cases related to poisoning, identification of individuals through fingerprint, serological analysis, DNA, arson, handwriting & signature analysis (letters, wills, notes, etc.), cyber forensics, forgery of documents like currency notes, stamp paper, passports, etc.

HR Institute of Forensic Science Research & Training is one of the most acknowledged Service Providers in Delhi, India which provides top class Forensic Science Training & Consultancy Services. The main reason behind entering in this domain is to empower the concerned personnel with the right set of knowledge and skills.

Founded in the year 2015, we have gained excellence in providing Training & Consultancy solutions to paralegals, scholars, investigators, interns and corporates. We offer skill based forensic science courses and trainings with the emphasize on their practical implementation and integrate the trainings with latest technology for getting more accurate & reliable results.

Training

The main objective of our company is to provide different forensic training programmes covers basic to advance level in the form of seminars, training sessions and workshops. These programs cover general topics for creating forensic awareness for the professionals doing investigations. It is also for the forensic experts who wants to learn more about the effective methods of collecting physical evidences. This mainly include the following:

  • Forensic Awareness Programs.
  • LLb program from "Krishna Law Academy, Alwar, Rajasthan".
  • Imparting in-depth knowledge in various disciplines of Forensic Science (Techniques, case studies, literature reviews, evaluation of new technologies, development of solutions as per organisation needs).
  • Would provide Forensic education kits (worksheets and materials, with instructions on their use for practical class).
  • Helping to learn the importance of ethics as when any evidence is manipulated, mishandled, or misinterpreted, it would eventually destroy a case which would ultimately lead to destruction of lives of people involved. It is therefore important for forensic scientists to have proper technical training, competency as well as good moral character.
